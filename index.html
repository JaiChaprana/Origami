What is Origami ?


Origami is the traditional Japanese art of folding paper into attractive shapes to make decorative objects. Ori meaning "folding", and kami meaning "paper". The small number of basic origami folds can be combined in a variety of ways to make intricate designs. The best-known origami model is the Japanese paper crane. In general, these designs begin with a square sheet of paper whose sides may be of different colors, prints, or patterns.
